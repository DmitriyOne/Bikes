2004-2005
2004
Creating of a brand Format is directly related with the name of Sergei Izhboldin. International Master of Sports, repeated the USSR and Russia champion, he was a real bicycle guru.
Sergei Izhboldin, 2004

He was interested in developing of cycling in Perm and Russia, the organization of the competitions, improving the design of cycles. Therefore, Sergey was invited to the company for the position of technical director to create a new product and a new brand of bicycles.

The main values of the project became a passion to win, rush forward and love for bicycles.

2004 — 2005
New bike brand was called Format. Under the leadership of Sergei Izhboldin there were developed the first design frames.

The main aim gets the creation of such bikes, which you can use to take part in races of the highest level, including the Olympics.

Design department in Perm is strengthened. The developed models are first tested.

2006
Format was first introduced to the market. The first serial products — three series of mountain bikes — are manufactured.
The brand Format begins in China

The total number of models is 18. The start of deliveries to Russian stores is begun; in this connection 2006 is considered the year of the official Format appearance.

There was established the first professional team in Russia Format Udmurtia. Specialization is cross-country discipline, part of the Olympic program.

Team of engineers joined Sergei Vlasov, repeated participant and winner of All-Russian competition in four-cross (4X) and downhill, vice president of the Cycling Federation of Perm region. The level of developments has grown substantially as a result of the use of 3D-design bicycle frames and components.

The project gets an international scale – the production of bicycles under the brand Format begins in China, at the factories of companies - partners of the project.

2007
The Format Udmurtia team gets the status of a professional team in the International Cycling Union (UCI) and successfully participates in European competitions.

Команда получила статус профессиональной в международном союзе велосипедистов (UCI) и успешно выступала на соревнованиях в Европе.

Конструкторское бюро работало над созданием новых моделей, осваивало новые дисциплины и направления, велись работы над разработкой рам с амортизатором.
2008
It’s continued development and promotion of the model range of the brand in Russia and China

The 14th of September is a black day in the history of the company and the Russian cycling. Plane crash interrupts the life of Sergei Izhboldin and CEO Alexander Ignatiev.
2009-2012
Promotion of the brand in the Russian market is suspended. However, the design department, the core of the project, is fully preserved and continues to work. The technological achievements of this period lay the foundation for further development.

During this period talented designers joined to the project staff. The result was a striking style that distinguishes Format from conventional bikes.

In Southeast Asia the Format brand continues to develop. In this period model range is updated with road bikes.

The Olympic Games in London. August 12, 2012 in the Olympic Hadleigh Park Eugene Pechenin starts in the cross-country race by bicycle of Russian brand. The rider of the Format Udmurtia team opens a new page of history - a dream came true about the use of Russian bikes in the race of the highest level!
2013
It was made a decision to resume sales in Russia.

A team of Russian Format Factory team DH is created. It tested the new bikes for downhill, appeared in the Russian Cup and the national championship. Format lineup is significantly updated. Production in Perm is preparing to release a models of 2014.
